GIRLS IN ICT

Igire Rwanda Organization runs a technical training program that targets young women from poor social and economic backgrounds in Kigali. Girls in ICT program helps girls who dropped out of the school and those who finished high school and do not continue to university with opportunity to learn computer skills, entrepreneurship skills and design thinking skills. Igire Rwanda Organization look for partnership to help girls to secure jobs and internship after the training. This training program is the first of its kind in Kigali and organization want to extend it to other parts of the country. During the training, women are trained on how to start their own small business and working together to easier access the funds from government and donors.

#SheCanCode Computer Programing for Girls in Rwanda

SheCanCode Coding basecamp for girls]- The project aims to train teenagersâ€™ girls in programing and web applications to empower girls and women through ICT. It is a three-month coding boot camp program designed to help girls navigate their career path in technology. The program is designed to help girls who completed high school to learn programing skills and consider pursuing their career in technology. Participants will learn programming languages such as CSS, HTML and JavaScript to prepare them to become front-end engineers. In addition, participants will learn design thinking and introduction to entrepreneurship to motivate them start their own tech business. The aim of the boot camp is to encourage more girls to pursue technical careers by empowering them with vital knowledge to facilitate their success. Again, this project will be decentralized to high schools where each year, 300 girls in 10 schools will learn programming.

Students learning HTML

In HTML, students are learning the basics of web-development and displaying contents on website. They learn different tag including the paragraph tag, heading tag, connecting to other links to other webpages; these among other things make up HTML.

Student learning CSS

With CSS, they are learning the basics of styling a page. They learn how to include colors, inserting pictures and generally how to design a website. This is important in helping them make more attractive websites.

JavaScript, dubbed as the language of the web. Students learn how to manipulate their webpages to make user friendly and interactive pages. They also learn the basics of programming in general and how to think like a programmer.
